# AI-Powered Coffee Customer Analytics & Recommendation System

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior over a 10-year period to predict future purchases and automate targeted marketing strategies for a coffee business.

## 📊 Features & Data Structure

The system processes a comprehensive dataset tracking customer demographics, detailed purchase histories, and store interactions:

*   **Customer Profiles:** Demographic data including age, gender, marital status, parental status, occupation, income, car ownership, social activities, and contact/address details.
*   **Product Details:** Granular data on coffee purchases (type, roast/production year, add-ons/customizations).
*   **Interaction History:** Logs of every customer touchpoint, including coffee purchases, pastry/accessory sales (mugs, equipment), and non-purchasing store visits (browsing).

## 🤖 Predictive AI Models

Using machine learning on the historical dataset, the project implements:
1.  **Purchase Prediction:** Forecasting which customers are most likely to buy coffee next.
2.  **Targeted Advertising:** An AI-driven recommendation engine that identifies the exact product type (specific coffee blends, bakery items, or accessories) to market to each individual customer profile.
